About CampusConnect

CampusConnect is a student-centered platform built to make life at NIT Trichy more collaborative and connected. It combines two major modules:

Skill Sharing

A space where students can create or join groups based on skills, interests, or academic branches. The platform encourages peer-to-peer learning, mentorship, and resource sharing, helping students to grow together.

Campus Navigation

Finding your way around campus is now easier than ever. The interactive map helps users locate departments, hostels, classrooms, and other facilities. It supports shortest path search and highlights nearby services for convenience.
