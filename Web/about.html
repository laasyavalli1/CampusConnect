About CampusConnect

CampusConnect is a student-driven initiative at NIT Trichy designed to make campus life smarter and more collaborative.

The platform brings together two major modules:

  • Skill-Sharing: Connect with peers, share resources, join branch/topic-specific groups, and find mentors.
  • Smart Navigation: Explore an interactive campus map that helps you find departments, hostels, and facilities efficiently.

Our mission aligns with the UNESCO Sustainable Development Goals, especially SDG 4 (Quality Education) and SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities & Communities).
